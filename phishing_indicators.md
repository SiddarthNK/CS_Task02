# Phishing Indicators Identified

## Major Indicators

### ✅ Spoofed Email Address
- Domain impersonation detected.

### ✅ Suspicious Links
- URL does not match the legitimate organization domain.

### ✅ Urgent Language
- Threatens account suspension within 24 hours.

### ✅ Generic Greeting
- Uses "Dear Customer" instead of recipient’s name.

### ✅ Header Discrepancies
- SPF/DKIM failures.
- Unauthorized sending server.

### ✅ Request for Sensitive Information
- Encourages the user to verify account details via a link.

---

## Final Assessment
The presence of multiple phishing traits confirms that the email is malicious and intended to steal user credentials.
